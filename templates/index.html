Strawberry Custard

1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
2. Mix strawberries with sugar.
3. Whisk eggs and cream, combine.
4. Bake for 25 minutes.
5. Serve chilled with blueberries.

Egg Italian Gravy

1. Boil eggs and peel.
2. Prepare tomato gravy with Italian spices.
3. Add eggs to gravy.
4. Simmer for 10 minutes.
5. Serve hot with bread or rice.

Chicken Kebab

1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices for 2 hours.
2. Skewer chicken pieces.
3. Grill or bake at 200°C for 20 minutes.
4. Serve hot with mint chutney.

Fresh Green Salad

1. Chop cucumber, lettuce, tomatoes, and bell peppers.
2. Add olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
3. Toss everything well.
4. Serve immediately chilled.

Chicken Biryani

..Wash rice and soak for 30 min.
1.Marinate chicken with yogurt, chili powder, turmeric, and salt.
2.Fry onions until golden, set aside half for garnishing.
3.Cook marinated chicken with onions, ginger-garlic paste, and spices.
4.Boil rice with salt until 70% cooked.
5.Layer chicken and rice, sprinkle mint & coriander.
6.Cover and cook on low flame for 15–20 min (dum style).
7.Serve hot with raita.
